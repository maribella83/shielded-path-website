Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 23, 2026

The Shielded Path ("we," "our," or "the app") is a free parental control application designed to help families protect children from harmful online content. We are committed to protecting the privacy of every user, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Collect

Information Stored Only on Your Device

The Shielded Path stores the following information locally on your device only. This information is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties:

  • Parent PIN — used to protect parental settings
  • Shield and blocking settings — your content blocking preferences
  • Custom blocked sites — websites you have chosen to block
  • Blocked app list — apps you have chosen to restrict
  • Screen time limits — daily usage limits per device
  • Device profiles — names and settings for devices you manage
  • SOS settings — parent phone number and child name for emergency alerts
  • SOS alert history — a log of when the SOS button was used
  • Weekly report settings — your email address for weekly reports
  • Location data — GPS coordinates stored locally and used only to display location to the parent and to assist with SOS alerts

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Browsing history
  • Personal photos or contacts
  • Financial information
  • Social Security numbers or government IDs
  • Any data from children under 13 to our servers

2. How We Use Information

All information collected by The Shielded Path is used solely to provide the app's safety features:

  • Location data is used to show parents where a protected device is located and to include a location in SOS alert text messages
  • Your email address is used only to send the weekly safety report you request — we do not use it for marketing
  • SOS alert logs are stored locally so parents can review past alerts

3. Third-Party Services

SMS and Email

When the SOS button is activated, the app opens your phone's native SMS application to send a text message to the parent's phone number. This uses your phone carrier's standard SMS service. We do not have access to the content of these messages. When you send a weekly report, the app opens your device's native email application. We do not have access to your email account or the content of emails sent.

Sex Offender Registry

The sex offender registry feature opens official government websites (nsopw.gov, familywatchdog.us) in your device's browser. We do not collect or store any information from these searches. Please review those websites' own privacy policies for information about how they handle your data.

Expo / React Native

The Shielded Path is built using Expo, a mobile development platform. Expo may collect anonymous crash reports and diagnostic data to help improve app stability. Please review Expo's privacy policy at expo.dev/privacy for details.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

The Shielded Path is designed to be used by parents to protect their children. We take children's privacy very seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • The app does not require children to create accounts or provide any personal information
  • All personal information (such as a child's name used in SOS alerts) is stored only locally on the device and is entered by the parent

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at maribelsaent@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

5. Data Security

All data stored by The Shielded Path is kept locally on your device and protected by your device's built-in security features (including device encryption and PIN/biometric lock). We do not operate servers that store your personal information, which means there is no central database that could be hacked or breached.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset App" feature within the app's settings
  • Deleting The Shielded Path from your device entirely

Deleting the app removes all locally stored data permanently.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information stored by the app (visible within the app's settings)
  • Correct any information (editable within the app's settings)
  • Delete all information (via the Reset App feature or by deleting the app)

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
