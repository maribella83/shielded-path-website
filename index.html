Why Families Trust Us

Protection That Works in the Background

No confusing dashboards. No tech degree required. The Shielded Path does the heavy lifting so you can focus on your family.

Active Shield Technology

Blocks harmful websites the moment your child tries to visit them — on any network, including cellular data.

Parent PIN Protection

Only you can turn off the shield. Your 4-digit PIN keeps kids from disabling protections when you're not looking.

Screen Time Limits

Our Safety Promise

Built for Families. Not for Profit.

Every decision in The Shielded Path was made with one question in mind: what would actually keep a child safer?

  • Free forever. No subscription, no trial period, no paywall. Protection shouldn't cost a monthly fee.

  • No ads, ever. We will never show your family advertisements or sell your data to third parties.

  • Your PIN stays on your device. It is stored locally and never sent to any server.

  • The CSAM layer cannot be turned off. Unlike other filters, this protection is permanently enabled in every version of the app.

  • Age-aware responses. Children and adults see completely different block screens — appropriate and honest for each person.

  • Built by a parent. This app was created by a mother who wanted something better for her own family.

🔴 FBI CyberTipline

When the CSAM warning screen appears, users are given direct access to report to the FBI at tips.fbi.gov.

🔵 NCMEC Integration

The National Center for Missing & Exploited Children CyberTipline (1-800-843-5678) is shown on every serious block screen.

🌐 IWF Blocklist Partnership

We are working with the Internet Watch Foundation to integrate their verified blocklist feed into the app's protection layer.

Simple Setup

Up and Running in Minutes

No IT experience needed. Just follow four simple steps.

01
Download the App

Install The Shielded Path on your phone from the App Store or Google Play.

02
Create Your PIN

03
